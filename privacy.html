Section 01

Data We Collect

Pawli collects only the data necessary to provide you with a comprehensive cat care management experience. Below is a transparent breakdown of each category of data we handle.

👤
A. Account Information (via Firebase Authentication)

Collected when you sign in with Apple or Google. Required to identify your account and sync your data.

  • Firebase User ID (unique identifier assigned by Firebase Auth)
  • Display name (provided by your Apple or Google account)
  • Email address (provided by your Apple or Google account)
  • Profile photo URL (if available from your sign-in provider)
  • Authentication provider (Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In)
💾

Stored in: iOS UserDefaults (app preferences), iOS Keychain (secure credential storage), and Firebase Authentication backend (cloud identity management).

🐾
B. Cat & Pet Data (user-created content)

All cat data is created entirely by you. You retain full ownership. See Section 3 for details on what stays local vs. what syncs to the cloud.

Cat Profiles

  • Name, breed, birthdate, gender, color
  • Profile photo (stored as compressed image)
  • Cat ID number, owner name, contact number

Health Information

  • Blood type, medications, allergies, health conditions
  • Vaccine records: name, date administered, next due date, vet name, batch number
  • Vet visit records: date, reason, vet name, clinic name, diagnosis, treatment, cost
  • Medical documents: title, type, OCR-extracted text — binary files stored LOCAL ONLY, never uploaded

Nutrition & Meals

  • Meal logs: food name, portion (grams), meal type, notes
  • Weight entries: weight in kg, notes, date
  • Food scan results: brand, product name, ingredients, nutritional data (protein, fat, fiber, moisture, calories, carbs, sodium, sugar %), safety analysis, barcode — scan images stored LOCAL ONLY

Reminders & Logs

  • Reminders: title, notes, due dates, repeat intervals, notification settings
  • Pet log entries: event type, notes, date, duration — photos stored LOCAL ONLY
📊
C. Analytics Data (PostHog)

We use PostHog to understand how the app is used so we can improve it. Analytics data is sent to PostHog's US-based servers.

  • Events tracked: app_launched, signed_in (with provider), signed_out, screen views
  • User properties sent: userId, name, email, authentication provider
  • PostHog host: US region (https://us.i.posthog.com)
✅

What we do NOT collect: Location data, contacts, clipboard contents, microphone audio, or any data beyond what is listed above.

Section 02

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve Pawli's cat care features. We do not monetize your data. Ever.

  • Provide core cat care management features (profiles, health records, nutrition tracking, reminders)
  • Sync your cat data across your devices via Google Firestore
  • Analyze food ingredients for cat safety using the Anthropic Claude AI API (only food product text is sent — no personal user data)
  • Send push notification reminders for care tasks, vaccines, and vet visits
  • Improve the app through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • Process and manage subscription purchases through Apple and RevenueCat
🚫

We do not and will never: Sell your data to third parties, use your data for advertising, share your personal information with any party not listed in this policy, or use your cat's health data for any purpose other than displaying it to you within the app.

Section 03

Cloud vs. Local Storage

We take a privacy-first approach to storage. Sensitive binary files (photos of documents, food scan images, pet log photos) are never uploaded to any server — they remain exclusively on your device.

☁️ Synced to Firestore
  • Cat profiles (with compressed photos)
  • Meal logs
  • Weight entries
  • Reminders
  • Vaccine records
  • Vet visit records
  • Medical document metadata (title + OCR text only)
  • Food scan metadata (not images)
  • Pet log metadata (not photos)
📲 Local Only — Never Uploaded
  • Medical document binary files (PDFs, images)
  • Food scan images
  • Pet log photos

🔑 Keychain Backup

Auth session, user settings, and cat data JSON are backed up in the iOS Keychain to survive app reinstalls, allowing seamless data recovery.

Section 05

Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data. Pawli is designed with user rights built into the app itself.

📤 Access & Export

Export all your data as a complete JSON file at any time using the built-in export feature in Settings.

🗑️ Delete Account

Delete your account permanently from within the app. All cloud data is removed from Firestore immediately and irreversibly.

✏️ Edit Your Data

Modify, correct, or update any cat profile, health record, or personal information directly within the app at any time.

🚪 Withdraw Consent

Uninstall the app and delete your account to remove all cloud data. Local data is cleared on uninstall.

✓ GDPR Compliant ✓ CCPA Compliant ✓ COPPA Compliant
ℹ️

GDPR: Data export, deletion, and userId isolation ensure compliance with EU data protection requirements. CCPA: We do not sell personal information. You have the right to know, access, and delete your data. COPPA: Pawli is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Section 06

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is stored and synced for as long as your account remains active.
  • Deleted accounts: All cloud data in Firestore is deleted immediately and permanently upon account deletion.
  • Local data: Cleared from your device when you uninstall the app.
  • Keychain data: Persists after app uninstall by design, enabling seamless data recovery if you reinstall. You can clear this by deleting the app and manually clearing your Keychain via iOS Settings.
  • Analytics data: Managed by PostHog per their retention policies. See PostHog's privacy policy for details.

Section 08

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption: All network traffic between the app and our cloud services is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Firebase Auth: Secure, industry-standard session management with token-based authentication.
  • iOS Keychain: Sensitive credentials and session tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain, which is hardware-protected on modern Apple devices.
  • Face ID App Lock: Optional biometric protection prevents unauthorized access to your app data.
  • UserId isolation: Firestore security rules ensure each user can only read and write their own data — other users' data is completely inaccessible.
⚠️

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Apple ID or Google account and to enable two-factor authentication on your sign-in provider.
